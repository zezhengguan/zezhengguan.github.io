TaBot is an AI-enabled robotic smart meeting table designed to foster equal dialogue experiences. By integrating multimodal sensor technology with AI algorithms, TaBot detects subtle shifts in atmosphere during multi-person conversations and responds with non-verbal dynamic changes that provide environmental cues to participants. This encourages participants to become more aware of their own conversational behavior and its impact on others.

The design demonstrates a potential future where AI seamlessly integrates into everyday objects.
