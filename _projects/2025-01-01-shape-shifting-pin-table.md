Shape-Shifting Pin Table is an actuated pin-array surface that renders digital content as dynamic, touchable physical relief, letting people perceive and manipulate data, terrain, and designs in tangible form. The project was developed with the Immersive Design Group and industry partners (DCBOLT, CBRE) for hands-on visualisation of the built environment, and was awarded an iF Design Award. [View the iF Design Award certificate (PDF)](/images/projects/Shape-shifting_Pin_Table3.pdf).
